🧠 High-Yield Facts

Dementia Facts

  • Alzheimer's disease accounts for 60-80% of dementia cases
  • MMSE scores: Normal (24-30), MCI (18-23), Dementia (<18)
  • Cholinesterase inhibitors: Donepezil, Rivastigmine, Galantamine
  • Memantine for moderate-severe Alzheimer's disease

💊 Pharmacology Pearls

AGS Beers Criteria 2023

  • Avoid: First-generation antihistamines (diphenhydramine)
  • Avoid: Benzodiazepines with long half-lives
  • Avoid: Non-selective NSAIDs
  • Caution: Anticholinergic medications

🚨 Emergency Pearls

Delirium Red Flags

  • Acute onset and fluctuating course
  • Inattention and disorganized thinking
  • Altered level of consciousness
  • CAM-ICU for ICU patients
